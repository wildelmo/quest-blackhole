Gargantua
A ray-traced black hole with a relativistic accretion disk. Put on a Meta Quest and press Enter VR — or on desktop: drag to look, WASDQE to fly, T tour, H stats. In VR: left stick fly, right stick turn/rise, A resume tour, B stats.
Star map: NASA/Goddard Space Flight Center Scientific Visualization Studio · Gaia DR2: ESA/Gaia/DPAC. Lensing technique after oseiskar/black-hole (MIT) and ebruneton/black_hole_shader (BSD-3) · Physics: James, von Tunzelmann, Franklin & Thorne 2015 (DNGR).
